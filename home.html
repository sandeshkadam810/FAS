FAQ

What is This Site for?

This site is a platform for sharing and discovering academic publications, research papers, and other scholarly works. It aims to provide a central hub for researchers and scholars to showcase their research and collaborate with others in their fields. Users can browse, search, and access a wide range of authentic academic content.

Show

Do we cover authentic work?

Yes, we strive to ensure that the content shared on this site is authentic and of high quality. All submissions are reviewed and verified by our team to maintain the integrity of the platform. Additionally, we encourage authors to provide proper citations and references to their sources to uphold academic standards.

Show

How to Log in?

To log in, click on the "Login" button located at the top right corner of the website. You will be directed to the login page where you can enter your username and password. If you don't have an account, you can create one by clicking on the "Sign Up" link and following the registration process. Once logged in, you can access your personalized dashboard and manage your publications.

Show

How long to add our research paper on the site?

The time it takes to add your research paper to the site depends on our review process. After submitting your paper, our team will carefully review it for authenticity and adherence to guidelines. Typically, the review process takes between 2 to 5 business days. If your paper meets the criteria, it will be published on the site, and you will receive a notification. In case of any delays or queries, you can contact our support team for assistance.
